Everyone is doing well with their part of work and I am glad to see us as a whole group who can help each other.

Qiurui Xu (Richard)

1. What went well?

We are all very efficient learners, and we finish our assigned tasks early to have a good pace. When differences of opinion arise, we also resolve them through communication and voting.

2. What could be improved?

I need to improve my presentation skills, and my group members often misunderstand me because of my inarticulate speech.

3. At least one thing that was surprising?

I was surprised at how efficient my group was in communicating. When someone is in trouble, others are always eager and prompt to help and answer questions.

4. At least one thing that you have learned about groups.

I learned from the group to listen to other people's perspectives. Their ideas then influence my thinking and broaden my horizons. Group members are not only studying partners. They are also your teachers.
