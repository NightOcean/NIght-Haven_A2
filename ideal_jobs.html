Ideal Jobs

The types of ideal jobs in our group can be broadly divided into three categories.

IT Engineer：Kemal，Dave

Software developer：Bruce，Kelvin

Management：Alex（IT manager），Richard（Digital product manager.

In all three positions we need good communication skills, and teamwork. And we need to know advanced programming skills in SQL, JavaScript, HTML and Java. These are used in database management, web development and core development, respectively. We also need to be familiar with various communications and application servers.

In all three professions, the difference is Management-oriented careers require more knowledge of business and management. And they should have vital leadership skill to influence and collaborate with and motivate followers and serve as an internal catalyst for strategic, progressive, and successful changes. Besides, they should have strong communication, presentation, collaboration, negotiation, and relationship-building skills to develop relationships with internal and external stakeholders.

Software engineers and software developers may feel like similar jobs, but there is a big difference, A developer. Their talents are usually concentrated in one area. There is no need for a "big picture". Therefore, software developers are primarily dedicated to developing code as part of the software development cycle. An engineer. Engineer’s design and plan the application of engineering principles to software development. Constantly aware of the 'big picture and with talents in many areas. The engineer can take on the developer's role, but the core focus of the engineer is on architecture.

Finally, the main difference between software engineering and software development is that the latter is responsible for engineering design, while the former focuses on creating functional programs.
