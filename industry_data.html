How do the IT-specific skills in your required skill set rank in terms of demand from employers?

- IT Manager requires a deep knowledge in all sorts of IT related skills, being able to use java, JavaScript, business management, building relationships and businesses process skills is essential to an IT manager.

- For Software developer/engineer, are problem solving skills, use of programming languages, time management skills, management, these skills are highly sought after.

- Digital Product Manager is very similar to a solutions architect, which is the most sought after job in IT field. Programming and coding, software development, software testing, problem solving, communication and teamwork are parts of the position are highly valued in the IT field and the general field as well.

How do the general skills in your required skill set rank in terms of demand from employers?

- IT Manager’s general skills include teamwork, management, and leadership as well as mentoring

- Software Developer/Engineer require general skills such communication skills, organisation, and teamwork in demand.

- - Digital Product Manager require general skills such as planning, trouble shooting and creativity.
